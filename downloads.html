Download InkBox OS

This page contains direct download links for InkBox OS. Once you have downloaded the file you need (see below), please follow the installation steps.
To check the model number, look at the bottom or back of your eReader.
If your model number is not on this page, unfortunately your eReader is not supported by InkBox at this time.
IMPORTANT: InkBox OS does NOT provide ANY WARRANTY at all and we are not responsible if you brick your device with one of the images below. Details here.
To see whether InkBox can be ported or not to your eReader, visit the Discord server or the GitHub repository.
